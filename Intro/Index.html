Family Time

Time is something that we cannot get back. Cherish every moment of the day that you've been allowed to have. Don't take it for granted. Connect with one another, put the electronics away and laugh often. Things may be different than the way it was when I grew up when it comes to family time, but it doesn't have to be.

No Phones Allowed

In a world full of technology, I like to take my children back to a time before computers, smartphones, and video games baecame the main household entertainment. Games such as:

  • Hide and go seek
  • I Declare War
  • Water balloon Fights
  • Puzzles
Thats just to name a few. If you really want to get competitive and get the family up and moving you can try some Minute to Win it Games based off the television game show.

Go exploring

So many people take for granted the beauty in their own backyard. Having lived in the same city for so many years you may think you've seen it all, but you would be surprised how much has changed and overlooked. For example the City of Chattanooga may seem like a small town but over the last few years, there's been major developments. Even with all of the new construction the natural beauties of the city have been preserved and plenty of campsites in and near the city of chattanooga.

Outdoor Chattanooga offers a range of activities around the city from
  • Kayaking
  • Biking
  • Hiking
  • Tubing
  • And so much more
Lets not forget to experience a drive in movie with family and it's only 20 minutes away from Chattanooga. Outdoor Wilderness Theater"> is an affordable 2 for 1 outdoor movie theater.
